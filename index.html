Rukhshan Haroon (he/him)

I am a fourth year Ph.D. student at Tufts University, USA. I am broadly interested in accessibility, generative AI, and human-computer interaction research. Recently, I have been redesigning instant messaging interfaces to incorporate assistive AI agents that facilitate smoother, more empathetic online interactions between autistic and non-autistic users.
