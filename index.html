Rukhshan Haroon

I am a second-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Tufts University, focusing on the intersection of user accessibility, systems, and machine intelligence. My goal is to build technologies that address the unique needs and challenges faced by people with disabilities, empowering them to lead more equitable and inclusive lives.

I am advised by Fahad R. Dogar.

Prior to Tufts, I completed my undergraduate degree in Computer Science from Lahore University of Management Sciences (LUMS), Pakistan. I was advised by Zartash Afzal Uzmi and Fareed Zaffar.
